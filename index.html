☆ Meet My Melody! ☆


My Melody is a Japanese character created by Sanrio in 1975. My Melody is known for her kind-hearted nature and gentle demeanor. Her core essence as a symbol of kindness, friendship, and positivity. She quickly took over Japan with her cute appearance and lovable personality. As a result, My Melody has appeared in various forms of media and merchandise all across the globe. She even has her own TV series!

.｡*ﾟ~ Origins ~ﾟ*｡.

My Melody originates from Sanrio, a Japanese company known for designing and producing cute characters. My Melody was designed by the Japanese illustrator and cartoonist, Motoo Fujiwara and was introduced in 1975. He initially created her as a mascot for the company's line of stationary products. Over time, My Melody's story expanded, and she became more than just a stationary mascot.

Today, My Melody remains an iconic character, adored by fans worldwide.

ﾟ+*:ꔫ:* Backstory *:ꔫ:*+ﾟ

My Melody's home is the world of Mariland. She lives in a forest with her younger brother, Rhythm, her parents, grandparents, and her best friends, My Sweet Piano and Flat.
